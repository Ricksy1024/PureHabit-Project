## Executive Summary

The implementation is broadly well-structured and substantially improved versus prior review cycles: sync conflict handling is transactional, ownership checks are present, and test evidence is strong (9/9 suites, 55/55 tests). However, one constitution-level blocker remains in auth enforcement: non-TOTP MFA claims can satisfy the callable auth gate, which violates the explicit TOTP-only requirement. Additional warning-level risks are concentrated in streak/scheduler scalability and test guardrails around the auth boundary.
